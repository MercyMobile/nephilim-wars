üîÆ Mage

Wielders of arcane power, mages command the forces of magic to devastating effect. While physically frail, their spells can turn the tide of battle.

Class Bonuses

Hit Points
d6 per level (lowest)
Primary Attributes
Intelligence
Armor Proficiency
None (can use Mage Armor spell)
Weapon Proficiency
Daggers, darts, slings, quarterstaffs, light crossbows

Special Abilities

  • Spellcasting: Access to the most powerful arcane spells
  • Arcane Recovery: Recover spell slots during short rest
  • Spell Mastery: Cast certain low-level spells at will
  • Signature Spells: Cast high-level spells without using spell slots

Spell Damage Types

  • Fire: Fireball, Flame Strike - High damage, area effect
  • Lightning: Lightning Bolt, Chain Lightning - Line and chain attacks
  • Ice: Ice Storm, Cone of Cold - Damage plus slow effects
  • Force: Magic Missile, Eldritch Blast - Guaranteed hits
  • Necrotic: Blight, Finger of Death - Life drain effects

Limitations

  • Low hit points - must avoid melee combat
  • Limited armor options - relies on defensive spells
  • Spell slots - powerful but limited resource
  • Concentration - many powerful spells require maintaining focus

‚öîÔ∏è General Combat Tips

Understanding Damage Types

  • Slashing: Swords, axes - effective against unarmored foes
  • Piercing: Arrows, spears - can penetrate armor
  • Bludgeoning: Clubs, hammers - effective against skeletons and constructs

Combat Modifiers

  • Advantage: Roll twice, take higher result (+5 equivalent)
  • Disadvantage: Roll twice, take lower result (-5 equivalent)
  • Cover: Half cover (+2 AC), Three-quarters (+5 AC), Full (can't be targeted)
  • Flanking: Advantage on attacks when allies surround enemy

Action Economy

  • Action: Attack, cast spell, dash, disengage, dodge, help
  • Bonus Action: Second Wind, healing word, off-hand attack
  • Reaction: Opportunity attack, shield spell, counterspell
  • Movement: Can be split before and after action
