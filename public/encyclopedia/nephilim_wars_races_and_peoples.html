The Peoples of the First World

The Three Creations

According to ancient texts and the Book of Enoch, three distinct orders of beings exist in the antediluvian world:

  1. The First Creation - Angels (Watchers): Created before the foundation of the earth, spiritual beings of immense power.
  2. The Second Creation - Humanity (Adam's Line): Made in the image of God, given dominion over the earth.
  3. The Third Creation - Nephilim (Forbidden Union): Offspring of Watchers and humans. Unnatural, powerful, and corrupted.

Classification System

PURE HUMAN LINES

Descendants of Adam through Seth or Cain. Shortest-lived but most blessed.

  • Sethites (Righteous)
  • Cainites (Corrupted)
  • Wanderers (Nomadic)
GIANT RACES

Offspring of Watchers. Supernatural size and strength.

  • Nephilim (1st Gen)
  • Rephaim (2nd Gen)
  • Anakim (Noble Giants)
HYBRID PEOPLES

Mixed bloodlines and corrupted arts.

  • Gibborim (Mighty Men)
  • Horim (Cave Dwellers)
  • Sorcerer Clans
CORRUPTED CREATURES

Animals twisted by experiments or possession.

  • Chimera (Mixed Beasts)
  • Dragons/Serpents
  • Demonic Fauna

Human Bloodlines

"And Adam lived an hundred and thirty years, and begat a son in his own likeness... and called his name Seth" - Genesis 5:3
Sethites
The Righteous Line

The descendants of Seth maintained the worship of God and preserved the history of the pre-fall world.

Attributes: +2 WIS, +1 Other

Starting Cost: 2 RP / 0 CP

Blessed Heritage: Advantage on Religion checks; easier prayer checks.

Divine Favor: 1/Long Rest, turn a failed save into a success.

Cainites
The City Builders

Descendants of the first murderer, builders of the first cities, masters of metallurgy and music.

Attributes: +2 Any, +1 Any

Starting Cost: 0 RP / 1 CP

Mark of Cain: Cannot be killed in single combat by vengeance seekers.

City Born: Advantage on social checks in urban settings.

Builder's Heritage: Proficiency with two artisan tools.

Wanderers
Nomadic Tribes

Those who rejected both the cities of Cain and the strictures of Seth, living in the wilds.

Attributes: +2 DEX, +1 WIS

Starting Cost: Variable

Survivalist: Advantage on finding food/water.

Swift Footed: Base speed 35 ft.

The Giant Races

The Watcher Bloodlines

Nephilim traits depend on their Watcher sire. Semjaza's line are kings; Azazel's are warriors and smiths.

Nephilim
First Generation Giants

Direct offspring of Angels and Humans. Titans of the ancient world.

Size: Large (12-15 ft)

Attributes: +4 STR, +2 CON, -2 DEX

Starting Cost: 0 RP / 3-5 CP

Angelic Resistance: Resistance to Radiant damage.

Insatiable Hunger: Must eat 4x normal food or gain Corruption.

Powerful Build: Count as Huge for lifting/grappling.

Sub-Species: Children of Azazel ▶

The Weapon Masters: Inherited the knowledge of war.

  • Master Smiths: Proficiency with all weapons/armor.
  • Forge-Born: Resistance to Fire.
  • War Tactics: +2 to Hit with metal weapons.

Hybrid Peoples

"And God saw that the wickedness of man was great... for all flesh had corrupted his way upon the earth."
Gibborim
Human/Giant Hybrids

The "Mighty Men of Old." Several generations removed from pure Nephilim, appearing as massive humans.

Attributes: +2 STR, +1 CON

Powerful Build: Count as one size larger.

Mighty Warrior: Proficiency with all martial weapons.

Heroic Feat: Perform incredible strength feats (GM discretion).

Horim
Cave Dwellers

Giant-kin adapted to underground life.

Attributes: +2 DEX, +1 WIS

Superior Darkvision: See 120ft in dark.

Sunlight Sensitivity: Disadvantage in direct sun.

Stone Cunning: Expertise in stone-related history/investigation.

Sorcerer Clans
Watcher-Taught Lines

Humans initiated into Watcher mysteries, altering their bloodlines forever.

Attributes: +2 INT, +1 CHA

Dark Insight: Passive Detect Magic.

Blood Magic: Sacrifice HP to cast spells without slots.

Watcher's Mark: Angels can sense you from 1 mile away.

Cultures & Society

Sethite Values

Monotheism, Patriarchal Authority, Separation from Corruption, Preservation of Pre-Fall History.

Cainite Values

Urbanization, Metalworking, Music, Personal Glory ("Making a Name"), Syncretism.

Nephilim Law

Might Makes Right. The strong eat the weak. Trophy taking, cannibalism, and god-king worship.
