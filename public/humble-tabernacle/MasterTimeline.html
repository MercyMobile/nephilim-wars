Nephilim Wars

Campaign Chronology

Canon (NASB)
Apocrypha / Lore
Archaeology
4174 BC

Creation Event

Formation of Adam and Eve. The Garden established.

Source: NASB Gen 1-2
3544 BC

Birth of Jared

"For in his days the angels descended." The name Jared means 'Descent'.

Source: NASB Gen 5:15 / Jubilees 4:15
~3500 BC

The Oath on Mt. Hermon

200 Watchers (Grigori), led by Semjaza, descend onto the summit of Mount Hermon. They swear a curse to take human wives.

Source: 1 Enoch 6:1-6
~3450 BC

Teaching of the Forbidden Arts

Azazel teaches metallurgy (swords/shields). Semjaza teaches sorcery. Baraqijal teaches astrology. The corruption of mankind begins.

Source: 1 Enoch 8:1-3
~3400 BC

Rise of the Nephilim

"The Nephilim were on the earth in those days... the mighty men who were of old, men of renown."

Source: NASB Gen 6:4
~3200 BC

The Great Corruption

The Giants consume the produce of men. When that fails, they turn to cannibalism and drink blood. The Earth cries out.

Source: 1 Enoch 7:3-6
3013 BC

Translation of Enoch

Enoch walks with God and is taken. Before this, he acts as Scribe of Judgment against the Watchers.

Source: NASB Gen 5:24 / Jubilees 4
Pre-Flood

Binding of the Arch-Watchers

Raphael binds Azazel in the desert of Dudael, covering him with jagged rocks. Michael binds Semjaza in the valleys of the earth.

Source: 1 Enoch 10 / DSS 4Q203
2518 BC (Year of Flood)

Death of Methuselah

The oldest man dies seven days before the waters rise. His name prophesied: "When he dies, it shall be sent."

Source: NASB Gen 5 / Seder Olam
2518 BC

The Great Flood

The fountains of the deep burst open. Destruction of the First Age and the physical bodies of the Nephilim (creating demons).

Source: NASB Gen 7
~2350 BC

Ebla Tablets

Archives mentioning the "Cities of the Plain" (Sodom, Gomorrah) before their destruction.

Origin: Tell Mardikh, Syria
~2200 BC

Nimrod & Babel

Nimrod, a "mighty hunter," establishes Babylon. Jewish tradition calls him a Gibbor (Giant/Tyrant) attempting to storm heaven.

Source: NASB Gen 10-11 / Josephus
~2080 BC

War of the Nine Kings

Chedorlaomer smites the Rephaim in Ashteroth-karnaim, the Zuzim in Ham, and the Emim in Shaveh-kiriathaim (All Giant Clans).

Source: NASB Gen 14:5
Tradition

The Survival of Og

Talmudic legend states Og of Bashan rode the roof of the Ark or survived due to his size/lineage. He becomes the King of Bashan.

Source: Talmud Niddah 61a
~1700 BC

Tall el-Hammam Event

Destruction layer confirming a directed airburst event that vaporized brick and boiled pottery in the Jordan Valley.

Origin: Jordan Valley
1446 BC

Report of the Spies

"We saw the Nephilim there... and we became like grasshoppers in our own sight."

Source: NASB Num 13:33
1406 BC

Defeat of Og

Moses defeats Og, King of Bashan. "Only Og remained of the remnant of the Rephaim. His bed was 9 cubits long (approx 13ft)."

Source: NASB Deut 3:11
DSS Era

The War Scroll (1QM)

Qumran text describing the final 40-year war between the Sons of Light and the Sons of Darkness (Belial), mirroring the ancient Watcher wars.

Source: Dead Sea Scrolls
33 AD

Victory over the Powers

Crucifixion and Resurrection. "He disarmed the rulers and authorities... triumphing over them."

Source: NASB Col 2:15
